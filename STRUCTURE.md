# NWO & Digital Harassment Research Directory Structure

This structure is designed for systematic OSINT and narrative analysis of NWO, cyberbullying, and gangstalking.

- `research/`
    - `entities/` (Profiles on individuals, organizations, and coordination nodes)
        - `DACH/` (Germany, Austria, Switzerland focus)
        - `GLOBAL/` (International connections)
    - `narratives/` (Analysis of specific myths and harassment patterns)
        - `NWO-origin/`
        - `coordinated-mobbing-tactics/`
        - `gangstalking-narrative-evolution/`
    - `technical_osint/` (Data from Chrome DevTools MCP)
        - `network_logs/`
        - `metadata_scans/`
        - `infrastructure_mapping/`
    - `legal/` (Court records, registry entries, stalking laws, official reports)
- `templates/` (Standardized markdown templates)
- `NARRATIVE_CHECK.md` (Central evidence tracker)
- `AGENTS.md` (Operational protocols)
