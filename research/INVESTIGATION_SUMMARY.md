## Executive Summary
The NWO (New World Order / Nie Wieder Online) represents a sophisticated, hierarchical criminal organization engaged in systematic cybermobbing and cyberstalking operations across Germany. Designated as a criminal organization by the BKA in September 2024, NWO conducts coordinated "Maskengames" targeting individuals for complete life destruction.

- **Hierarchy:** Structured with clear roles including "self-proclaimed spokesperson"

- **Communication:** Primarily Discord-based coordination

### 3. Law Enforcement Response

- **Targets:** Ten suspected ringleaders

### 4. Documented Cases

- **Media Coverage:** Extensive coverage across major German media outlets
- **Public Awareness:** ARD/WDR documentary "Das Cybermobbing-Kartell" (October 16, 2024)
