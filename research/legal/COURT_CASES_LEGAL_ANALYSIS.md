## Legal Framework Analysis

### German Criminal Code (StGB) Applications

#### Primary Criminal Charges
1. **§ 129 StGB** - Formation of Criminal Organization
   - **Applied to:** NWO leadership structure
   - **Evidence:** Hierarchical organization, coordinated actions
   - **Penalty:** Up to 5 years imprisonment

2. **§ 238 StGB** - Stalking (Nachstellung)
   - **Applied to:** Systematic harassment campaigns
   - **Evidence:** Repeated unwanted contact, threats
   - **Penalty:** Up to 3 years imprisonment

3. **§ 202a StGB** - Violation of Confidentiality
   - **Applied to:** Data breaches, privacy violations
   - **Evidence:** Hacked accounts, data theft
   - **Penalty:** Up to 2 years imprisonment or fine

4. **§ 303b StGB** - Computer Sabotage
   - **Applied to:** Account hacking, platform interference
   - **Evidence:** Technical interference with digital services
   - **Penalty:** Up to 3 years imprisonment

5. **§ 263 StGB** - Computer Fraud
   - **Applied to:** Fraudulent orders, financial crimes
   - **Evidence:** Economic damage to victims
   - **Penalty:** Up to 5 years imprisonment

### Constitutional Law Considerations
- **§ 86a StGB** - Use of Symbols of Unconstitutional Organizations
- **§ 130 StGB** - Incitement to Hatred (Volksverhetzung)
- **Investigation Ongoing:** BKA examining constitutional symbol usage
